Hairdressing and Beauty Therapy

Welcome to the Department of Hairdressing and Beauty at Madogo Vocational Training Centre. This department is dedicated to nurturing creativity and technical expertise in the beauty industry. Our programs are designed to provide a comprehensive education in both traditional and modern beauty techniques, ensuring our trainees are well-prepared for the dynamic and competitive beauty market.

Courses Offered:

    Hairdressing Beauty: Master the art of hairstyling, treatments, and modern trends in hair care.
    Henna Design: Learn traditional and contemporary henna application techniques to create stunning designs for all occasions.

Our Training approaches

Our training approach combines theoretical learning with hands-on practical experience to ensure a comprehensive understanding of ICT concepts and their real-world applications. We conduct two types of assessments:
    1. Formative Assessment: Continuous evaluation to track your progress throughout the course.
    2. Summative Assessment: Administered by accredited bodies such as CDAAC, NITA, and KNEC to validate your competencies.
After completing the training, trainees proceed to a three-month industrial attachment, where they gain valuable industry exposure and practical experience. Upon successful completion, trainees graduate, ready to take on the challenges of the ICT field. Whether you're looking to kickstart your ICT career or enhance your existing skills, we are here to guide and support you on your journey.
