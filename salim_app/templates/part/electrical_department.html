Electrical and Electronic Engineering

We are delighted to welcome you to the Electrical and Electronic Engineering Department at Madogo Vocational Training Centre. Here, we are committed to empowering our trainees with the technical knowledge and hands-on skills needed to excel in the dynamic and ever-evolving field of electrical and electronic engineering. Our department offers accredited courses in Electrical Installation and Electrical Wireman, tailored to meet industry standards and prepare you for a rewarding career. With a blend of theoretical and practical training, supported by state-of-the-art facilities, our programs are designed to foster innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Whether you're here to begin your journey in the electrical field or to enhance your existing expertise. We have the following courses

  • Electrical wireman
  • Electrical Installation
    • our courses are examined by the following examination bodies;
  • KNEC
  • CDACC
  • NITA
    • After completing the training, trainees proceed to a three-month industrial attachment, where they gain valuable industry exposure and practical experience. Upon successful completion, trainees graduate, ready to take on the challenges of the technology field. Whether you're looking to kickstart your Electrical career or enhance your existing skills, we are here to guide and support you on your journey. Welcome to a learning environment where innovation meets opportunity.
