Welcome to Madogo Vocational Training Centre

Welcome to Madogo Vocational Training Centre (VTC), a leading institution in technical and vocational education located in Bura-Tana, Tana River County. Guided by our motto, "Skills for Better Living," we are dedicated to transforming lives through practical skills and innovative training programs.

Vision: To become a globally competitive institution in industry and business by providing quality skills to trainees.

Mission: Provision of a workforce with requisite knowledge, skills, and a positive attitude to help in the economic development of the world.

Core Values:

    1. Quality Training: We ensure that our programs meet the highest standards of competence and innovation.
    2. Inclusivity: We embrace diversity and promote equal opportunities for all learners.
    3. Integrity: We uphold transparency, accountability, and ethical practices in all our operations.
    4. Collaboration: We work with stakeholders to ensure our trainees meet industry demands.

Why Choose Us?

Modern Facilities: Our institution is equipped with state-of-the-art resources to enhance hands-on learning.

Competency-Based Education: We focus on practical, market-driven skills to make our graduates industry-ready.

Community Impact: We contribute to regional and global development by producing a skilled and professional workforce.
