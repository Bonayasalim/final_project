His Execellecy the deputy Governor Hon. Mahat Loka awarding Certificates during our 1st graduation ceremony

Thursday, 14th November 2024

On 14th November 2024, we proudly held our 1st Graduation Ceremony, celebrating the success of over 60 graduates in various fields of study. The momentous occasion was graced by His Excellency the Deputy Governor of Tana River County, Hon. Mahat Loka, highlighting the importance of technical education in driving regional development.

Luncheon with the CECM for Education and the area Member of County Assembly

Monday, 8th July 2024

On 8th July 2024, MVTC hosted a Luncheon with the CECM for Education and the Area Member of County Assembly, fostering partnerships and support for advancing vocational training in the region. This event underscored the value of collaboration in empowering our trainees and improving regional development.

The Subcounty Deputy County Commissioner (DCC) led a tree planting activity

Thursday, 14th November 2024

During the 1st graduation ceremony of Madogo Vocational Training Centre on 14th November 2024, the Subcounty Deputy County Commissioner (DCC) led a tree planting activity as part of the celebrations.

The Madogo Ward Member of County Assembly (MCA)

Thursday, 14th November 2024

During the 1st graduation ceremony of Madogo Vocational Training Centre on 14th November 2024, the Madogo Ward Member of County Assembly (MCA) led a tree planting initiative to mark the special occasion. This activity underscored the MCA's commitment to environmental conservation and community development, aligning with the ward's efforts to promote sustainability.
